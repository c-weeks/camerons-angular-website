Chocolate

Besides baking, I LOVE chocolate. After trying all of the small batch dark chocolates I wanted to try making some of my own. The one catch is that it requires a melanger/refiner which is hard to find in a size that makes sense for an apartment and is a reasonable price. For Christmas, I got one and I have had a blast making my own choclate. It is a laborious process, but it is absolutely worth it. The resulting chocolate is delightful.
